About Canggu Beach

Canggu Beach, located on Bali's southwestern coast, is a popular destination known for its stunning black sand, consistent surf, and vibrant beach culture. Renowned among surfers and beachgoers alike, Canggu offers ideal conditions for both seasoned pros and beginners looking to catch their first wave. The picturesque sunsets and lively beach clubs create a relaxed yet energetic atmosphere, making it the perfect spot for those seeking adventure and relaxation.

Beyond its natural beauty, Canggu has become a hub for digital nomads and travelers. With a variety of trendy cafes, healthy eateries, and yoga studios, visitors can enjoy a laid-back lifestyle that blends modern comforts with traditional Balinese charm. Whether you're exploring the scenic rice fields, savoring a meal with ocean views, or enjoying the local nightlife, Canggu Beach offers a unique experience that captures the essence of Bali.

Nasi Campur

Nasi Campur Bali is a traditional Balinese dish featuring white rice served with an assortment of sides like ayam betutu, sate lilit, lawar, and sambal matah, offering a rich and authentic taste of Balinese flavors.

Ayam Betutu

Ayam Betutu is a flavorful Balinese dish made from chicken that is marinated with a blend of traditional spices, then wrapped in banana leaves and slow-cooked, resulting in tender, aromatic meat with a rich, spicy taste.

Babi Guling

Babi Guling is a traditional Balinese dish consisting of a whole roasted pig, marinated with a blend of spices and herbs, and slow-cooked over an open fire, resulting in crispy skin and tender, flavorful meat.

Plecing Kangkung

Plecing Kangkung is a refreshing Balinese salad made from boiled water spinach, served with a spicy sambal made of chili, tomato, and lime, offering a bright, tangy, and slightly fiery flavor.

Lawar

Lawar is a traditional Balinese dish made from a mix of finely chopped vegetables, grated coconut, and minced meat, often seasoned with rich spices and sometimes mixed with fresh blood for added flavor, offering a unique and savory taste.

Sate Lilit

Sate Lilit is a Balinese satay made from minced meat, typically chicken or fish, mixed with grated coconut and spices, then wrapped around bamboo sticks and grilled, delivering a smoky, flavorful taste with a hint of sweetness.
